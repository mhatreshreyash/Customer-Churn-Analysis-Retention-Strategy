# Customer-Churn-Analysis-Retention-Strategy


## Project Overview

Customer churn is a major challenge for telecom companies because losing customers directly affects revenue, profitability, and long-term business growth. This project performs an end-to-end business analysis on telecom customer data to identify the factors contributing to customer churn and provide data-driven recommendations for improving customer retention.

The analysis combines statistical techniques, customer segmentation, exploratory data analysis, and business intelligence reporting to support strategic decision-making.

---

## Business Problem

The telecom company is experiencing customer attrition and wants to understand:

* Why customers are leaving
* Which customer segments are at risk
* How churn impacts business performance
* What actions can reduce churn and increase retention

---

## Project Objectives

* Analyze customer behavior and churn patterns
* Identify key drivers of customer churn

## Key Findings

### Finding 1

Customers with month-to-month contracts have the highest churn rate.

### Finding 2

Customers with lower tenure are more likely to leave.

### Finding 3

Higher monthly charges increase churn probability.

### Finding 4

Electronic check users show higher churn behavior.

### Finding 5

Long-term customers demonstrate stronger loyalty.

---

## Business Recommendations

### Recommendation 1

Offer discounts for annual and long-term contracts.

### Recommendation 2

Develop onboarding programs for new customers.

### Recommendation 3

Implement customer loyalty and reward programs.

### Recommendation 4

Use predictive analytics to identify high-risk customers.
